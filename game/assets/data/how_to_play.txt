Controls:
WASD - to move
SHIFT - to sprint
SPACE or Right Mouse Click - dash
Left Mouse Click - shoot
Esc - menu

Goal:
Snowmen attack and steal gifts prepared for little children! 
Shoot 'em and save gifts!

Squirrel will help you by making gifts each 35 seconds.

After clearing each wave go to the center of the arena to 
a deer to get your rewards!

Advice: upgrade damage
